  • 2.5. Why do I have a connection problem?

    This is a very technical question.

    The EnglishMaster uses anyone of port 7000 or 443 (https) or 80 (http) for Internet communication. Please make sure your Internet provider open one of them.

    Some Internet providers open port 80 only and convert all requests into http requests or cache all responses. In this case, you may not use EnglishMaster.

    For general users, you don't need to be worried about these details. The EnglishMaster is as simple as clicking buttons.

  • 4.1. How do I get CHARGE usage mode?

     NOTE: Before you change to CHARGE usage mode, please make sure you have enough fund in your account. Otherwise please deposit fund into your account first.
    1. In the EnglishMaster application's main screen, click on "ACCOUNT" button.
    2. You are prompted to login: enter your registered email address and your password to login.
    3. Choose "Current Setting and Usage" on "User Account" window and then click on "CONTINUE".
    4. Click on "Change" button in 'Current Mode' line of 'Your Current Settings' group on "User Account - Current Setting and Usage" window.
    5. Read the "CHANGE MODE" help first and then select an available mode that you like at 'You new mode will be:' line (All modes except Free are charged mode).
    6. Click "Yes" button. You will see a message to tell you that the change is successful or failure.
    7. After you successfully buy a "CHARGE" usage mode, return to the main screen and click the "CHANGE MODE" button and make sure that the upper black title is "CHARGE MODE".
    8. Now, you can start to use the CHARGE mode you have chosen.

    NOTE:
    - Change your current mode to one of the CHARGE modes. Your account is charged by the price shown.
    - If you have any un-used charge time remaining, it will be carried over.

  • 4.2. How do I get a future CHARGE usage mode?

     The steps to get a future CHARGE usage mode are almost the same as the steps to get a current CHARGE usage mode described above, except: You click on "Change" button in 'Future Mode' line of 'Your Current Settings' group on "User Account - Current Setting and Usage" window.

    NOTE:
    Your account balance is not changed when you change your future usage mode because the usage has not applied yet. You are only charged when you really use it.

  • 4.3. What is the future CHARGE usage mode for?

     The future CHARGE usage mode is for your convenience to have an uninterrupted service. By setting the future mode, our system will automatically update your usage service on monthly basis and all your un-used charge time will be carried over. By default, your future usage mode is 'Free Mode'.

  • 4.4. Can I change mode from a CHARGE mode to FREE Mode?

     Yes, you can. But we strongly don't suggest you to do that. In this case, you will lose all your un-used CHARGE mode time.

  • 4.5. How does EnglishMaster manage time?


     All usage time within EnglishMaster is monthly based.

    All usage modes provide some free usage time. But only charged modes provide charged time which gives you full access to all content resources. Free time and charge time are counted separately.

    Within one month, if you use up one usage time, you can not access this usage mode any more. So you have to get another CHARGE mode as described above (see How do I get CHARGE usage mode?)

    When the usage time reaches one month boundary, the system resets you account based on your future usage mode (by default, it is 'free mode').

    If you want a continuous service without losing your un-used charge time, we recommend you to set your future usage mode to one of charged modes. Otherwise, you may lose your remaining charge time, see Why do I lose all un-used remaining charge time?

    You can also learn How can I prevent from losing un-used remaining charge time?

  • 4.6. Why do I lose all un-used remaining charge time?

     All usage time within EnglishMaster is monthly based. If your current usage is expired, you current usage will be reset based on 'Future Mode' setting. Therefore, if your 'Future Mode' setting is 'free mode', you are going to lose all un-used charge time.

  • 4.7. How can I prevent from losing un-used remaining charge time?

     There are two ways to prevent you from losing un-used remaining time.

    - Set you 'Future Mode' at one of charged mode and make sure you have enough fund to buy this mode in the future time.
    - Or, get a CHARGE usage mode before the current one expired.
