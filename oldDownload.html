Download and Install Instructions

    * Note: If you installed the latest version 2.00 before and want to go back to the old version 1.04, you have to remove the installed version 2.00 first by going to "Control Panel" -> "Add/Remove Programs" -> "English Master" -> "Remove".

  1. Click the Download button to begin the download. The installer file appears in a Save As dialog box. Its name is "emsetupT5.exe" or "emsetupxp.exe".
  2. Click Save.
  3. Be sure to note where the file is going to be saved.
  4. Double-click emsetupT5.exe or emsetupxp.exe you just saved.
  5. Follow the on-screen instruction after the Welcome screen displayed.

If you have installation questions or need help troubleshooting EnglishMaster, go to our Support Center.

After Installation

  1. After successfully installing your EnglishMaster, launch the application by clicking the icon on your desktop.
  2. Click "Account" button.
  3. Use your real email to sign up by clicking "SIGNUP" button at the bottom of the "User Account" window.
  4. The system will send you an email with your initial password.
  5. Then you can use your email and the password to login.
